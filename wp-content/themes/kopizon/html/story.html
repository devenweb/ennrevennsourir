Our Story

Founded with love, driven by hope.

Over 10 Years of Impact

Enn Rev Enn Sourir was born from a simple yet powerful dream: that every child in Mauritius, regardless of their background, deserves access to the highest standard of healthcare. What started as a small group of passionate individuals has grown into a nationwide movement of hope.

Our mission is to bridge the gap in paediatric care, providing financial and emotional support to families battling severe illnesses. We believe that no parent should have to choose between their child's health and their financial stability.

Our Vision

A future where every child in Mauritius has the health and happiness they deserve.

Our Mission

To provide essential financial aid and emotional support to children suffering from cancer and serious health conditions.

Our Values

Compassion, Transparency, and Excellence in all our efforts to save lives.

Enn Rev Enn Sourir believes that all children deserve to have the same opportunities to receive the best health services, whether through online donations or volunteering, your help is valuable.
