About Our Charity

From the first idea to the final outcome

Since 2016, Enn Rev Enn Sourir has been dedicated to ensuring all children receive the best medical treatment, regardless of financial barriers. We facilitate access to specialized care that is otherwise inaccessible, ensuring every child has a second chance at life.
