Success Story Medical Journey

Maely was diagnosed with Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia, a battle that lasted several years. Today, we are proud to share that she is in full remission and has returned to her normal childhood activities.

Her journey was supported by hundreds of donors who provided the funds for her chemotherapy, hospital stays, and specialised care in India. Maely's story is a testament to what we can achieve when we come together as a community.

"Maely is our little warrior. Seeing her smile again is the greatest reward for all our efforts." - Maely's Parents
