Paediatric Cancer Scheme

Home / Campaigns / Current Project
  • Description
  • Updates
  • Donors

In collaboration with the Ministry of Finance, Enn Rev Enn Sourir manages the 'Paediatric Cancer Scheme' to provide specialized oncology care for all children on the island. We ensure that no financial barrier stands between a child and their life-saving chemotherapy or surgery.

How your donation helps:

  • Fund critical chemotherapy sessions for pediatric patients.
  • Facilitate international medical transport for complex surgeries.
  • Provide 24/7 psychological and psychosocial support to families.
